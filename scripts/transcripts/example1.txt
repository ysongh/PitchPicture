Okay so here's the onboarding flow I'm imagining. A new user lands on the marketing site and clicks Get Started. We send them to a sign-up page where they pick Google or email. If they pick Google we just OAuth them in and drop them on the dashboard. If they pick email we send a verification link, they click it, and then they land on the dashboard. But the first time anyone lands on the dashboard we want a checklist — like, three things they need to do before the product feels useful. Connect a data source, invite a teammate, and create their first project. Once they've done all three the checklist disappears and they see the normal dashboard. If they bail halfway through, next time they come back the checklist is still there with the remaining items.
