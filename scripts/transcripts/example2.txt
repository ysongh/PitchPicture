I've been thinking about why our retention drops off in week two. There's a few threads here. One is just that the core loop isn't tight enough — people don't have a reason to come back daily, it's more of a weekly thing for them. Related to that, our notifications are pretty generic, they don't actually pull anyone back. Another angle is onboarding — we throw a lot at people in the first session and I think they leave without really understanding the value. And then there's the social piece, which is that right now the product is fundamentally single-player, so there's no network pulling them back in. Oh and pricing too — the free tier might actually be too generous, people get what they need and don't have a reason to upgrade or engage more deeply. I keep going back and forth on which of these is the biggest lever.
